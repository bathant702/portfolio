#0.1 About_Me

Hello and welcome. My name is Tim Bathan and I make stuff. My interests for web development goes as far back as the 90s-00s, when code new and Flash games were at their peak. After being taught the basics of HTML and CSS, I always found myself exploring and tinkering with their code, finding out why it displayed "this" component or where "that" component was being stored.

Fast-forward to today: I am a recently graduated General Assembly student from the Full Immersive Software Engineering course. While I've only had a taste of what I can do, my projects reflect my views on web development: passionate, practical, and a bit of fun.

Here are some of the technologies I've used in creating my projects:

  • Frontend
    • HTML, CSS, Javascript, React
  • Backend
    • Express.js, Node.js, Python
  • Database
    • MongoDB, SQL, PostgresSQL
  • Design and Layouts
    • Affinity Designer, Photo, & Publisher
