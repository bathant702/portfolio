#0.1 About_Me

Hello and welcome. My name is Tim Bathan and I make stuff. My interests for web development goes as far back as the 90s-00s, when code new and Flash games were at their peak. After being taught the basics of HTML and CSS, I always found myself exploring and tinkering with their code, finding out why it displayed "this" component or where "that" component was being stored.
